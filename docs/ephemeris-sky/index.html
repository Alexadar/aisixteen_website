Ephemeris Sky

The sky, computed for any moment.

Turn any moment into a precise picture of the solar system. Ephemeris Sky computes real geocentric planetary positions, the aspects between them, and a live chart wheel for any date and time — and tracks each planet’s synodic cycle so you always know when the next retrograde begins.

Questions

Does Ephemeris Sky work offline?+

Yes. Ephemeris Sky runs entirely on your device, so it works with no internet connection.

Is there an account or subscription?+

No. There's no sign-up, no login, and no subscription — just download Ephemeris Sky from the App Store and open it.

What data does Ephemeris Sky collect?+

None. It collects, stores, and transmits zero personal data — nothing ever leaves your device.

Which devices are supported?+

Ephemeris Sky is available for iPhone, iPad & Mac.
